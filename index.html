  • The Structural Re-parameterization Universe

    • GitHub (2000 stars) | Paper

    I proposed Structural Re-parameterization, a methodology that converts a specific structure into another via transforming the parameters. I started to investigate into it since ACNet (ICCV 2019) and continued to work on it during my internship with Dr. Xiangyu Zhang. I started to use Structural Re-parameterization to term this methodology since RepVGG (CVPR 2021) and Diverse Branch Block (CVPR 2021). ResRep (ICCV 2021) is a SOTA channel pruning method that uses Structural Re-parameterization.
